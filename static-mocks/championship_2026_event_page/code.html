Welcome to Krakow

We are thrilled to invite your team to the XII Championship 2026. This year marks a special anniversary for our association, bringing together over 50 teams from across Europe to compete in the historic city of Krakow.

Prepare for two days of high-energy performances, world-class judging, and an unforgettable atmosphere. Whether you are competing in All-Girl, Coed, or Performance Cheer divisions, this event promises to be the highlight of the 2026 season.

Travel & Accommodation

hotel

Accommodation Information

We do not have exclusive partnerships with specific hotels this year, allowing teams the flexibility to choose accommodation that best suits their budget and preferences. The venue is located in the Nowa Huta district, which offers several affordable options.

For teams wishing to stay closer to the city center, we recommend looking for hotels near the main tram lines leading to Nowa Huta. Large groups are advised to book well in advance, as March is a busy season in Krakow.

lightbulb Travel Tip

We recommend using Uber or Bolt for group transport if not using a chartered bus, as it is often cost-effective in Krakow.
